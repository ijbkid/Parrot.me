As the average internet users attention span continues to decline, apps like Vine and Instagram Video continue to grow in popularity. We started ClipMe which allows you create and share short duration (15-second) videos, but with a twist.

Our app takes a more collaborative approach – users can record the first few seconds of a video and then invite friends to complete it. Once the collaborative video is ready, it can be shared on Facebook, Twitter and anywhere on the web. Research shows that the presence of a circle of friends on ClipMe is essential to encouraging collaborative video creation
